About VECG

VECG or known as (Virtual Enviroments and Computer Graphics) is one of the most highly rated research area in UCL. Using multiple approach like algorithm and interaction, the researchers have been producing phenomenal publications including Texture Stationarization (2017) and Computational Network Design from Functional Specifications (2016).

The group does not only have a good foundation in working on 3D interaction and digital heritage, but also successfully being pioneer atstudy of presence as a measure of the effectiveness of immersive virtual environments. As mixed reality is growing tremendously in gaming industry, UCL researchers are keen of exploring potential of augmented reality and method to collaborate in shared ubiquitous environments.

We have strong academic appointments, being recruited by MIT, Princeton and ETH Zurich for collaboration. Our ultimate goal is making virtual reality functioning in a given application context and with given resources, hence solving a lot of resources and financial problems in creating a virtual world as large, or even larger than the world.
