What is Virtual Enviroments and Computer Graphics?

Throughout your life you may have some moments where things so crazy happen you have to take a step back and ask yourself, what is reality? What makes my expereince real and how do I know I am living in the real world? That is the question we ask ourselves at VECG every day. In our quest to create the perfect virtual reality, they key is to understand what makes our reality real. We have all put on a cheap VR headset and despite the fancy looking graphics known that we were simply viewing a simulated world. Our goal is to transport you to a real one. Crowd simulation, space filling, realistic character rendering, visibilty, it is the little details that make a virtual enviroment seem real. It is not an easy task, but perhaps one day, reality and simulation won't be all that far appart.
