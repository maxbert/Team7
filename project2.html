Reflectance and Natural Illumination from Single-Material Specular Objects Using Deep Learning

  1. Home
  2. Research
  3. Reflectance and Natural Illumination


Project Description

The research is aimed at estimating information about reflectance ad illumination. The method uses a very big set af data to learn on it where shape, reflectance and illumination are not known.
The method is completed in two steps.
The first part is dedicated to estimate the object's reflectance map. Here a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is used to complete this stage.
The second step decomposes the object into reflectance and illumination. Here CNN is used to reconstruct reflectance parameters and spherical illumination maps.
This approach is proven to be effective by quantitative and qualitative evaluation.

Authors:

U.Leuven)

Impact

This method facilitates image-based rendering applications. It can be successfully used with complex objects. New deep learning architectures were developed. This approach outperforms the works created earlier, allowing to highlight the deep learning's potential in inverse rendering tasks and computer graphics. Much more is still to come!
